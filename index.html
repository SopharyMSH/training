About

At Rotati IT Training we take pride in our software development training quality. We want to help you turn your team into a high quality, high volume output team that feel passionate about their work.

We do this though intensive, interactive, hands-on (and fun!) training.

We can help you bring your team up to speed with the latest technology, standards, approaches and practices. We can make sure that you are not left behind!

If you answer ‘yes’ to any of the questions below, this it’s likely you will benefit from a training session from us. Even a 1 day training session will make a noticable difference!

  • Do you employ or contract software developers?

  • Do you feel that there could be a ‘better way’, a faster way to develop your products?

  • Are you feeling frustrated by the quality of your software deliverables?

  • Do you often feel that your software developer team would benefit from training?

  • Does your team lack some of the technical skills for modern software development?

  • Do your team use outdated software tools and approaches?

  • Do you wish you could be more agile and use project management approaches like Scrum?

  • Does your team lack essential security knowledge?

    • Course Delivery

    Modern and relevant content

    All our courses are of the highest quality and are produced using the latest approaches, technology and standards to ensure that we are delivering the best and most relevant material. We train software development teams with the real world in mind and we put the theory into practice - always.

    Hands On and engaging

    All our courses includes plenty of hands on, try it for yourself material, that is conducted during class time. All software required is provided by the trainer and any setup required will be instructed before the class begins so that we can start right away without any cumbersome, time consuming technical setup.

    Satisfaction

    We are not satisfied until all the software team have fully understood and grasped all the course content. We encourage this through engagement and demonstrations of understanding through practical, hands on exercises.

    Content

    A single training session will cover one of the specific Subject Areas listed above. We can prepare specific content within each Subject Area to suit your needs. Contact us to get the discussion going!

    Trainers

    All are trainers are highly skilled and knowledgeable in their subject area. They are well practiced professionals with relevant experience in the industry.

    Location - on site or anywhere you choose!

    We deliver the course on site, at your premises. If you wish to have the course conducted outside your premises then we request you make the necessary arrangements. Travel and accommodation costs may apply (see FAQ for more details).

    Training Retreats

    Some companies value training delivery when its made outside of the office and even outside of the city altogether. We are perfectly able to travel to any remote location to conduct training as required. However, travel and accommodation costs need to be covered as required.

    Preparation

    Before a course begins we need to know:

    • Which one of the Subject Areas you wish to cover in the course
    • Optionally, what specific subjects within the chosen Subject Area you would like us to cover. We can arrange a meeting to discuss this detail, if required.
    • How many candidates will attend the course.

    We will then prepare an agreement based on the above decisions. Once the agreement is signed we will request a 40% down payment and then we will arrange the course.

    Pricing

    Our pricing is simple, we have a set day rate of $500 USD + VAT no matter how many candidates attend of the course.
